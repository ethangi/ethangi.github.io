1、并列连词

  1. and, but, or, so
  2. not … but ..
    not only
    both
    .. and
    neither.. nor
    either
    Or
    .. rather than
    … as well as …

2、并列连词前后语法结构需要一致

  • 正确: Students should study diligently rather than being addicted to
    computer games.
  • 错误: Students should study diligently rather than be addicted to computer
    games.

3、副词和介词短语（状语） 不是井列连词，不能并列两个成分

  • 因为: because of / due to / owing to / thanks to / on account of / as a result of / as a
    consequence of
  • 所以: thus, therefore, as a consequence, as a result
  • 转折: however, nevertheless, conversely, in contrast, on the contrary, on the other hand
  • 让步: admittedly, despite / in spite of
  • 递进：even
  • 无论: regardless of /disregarding
  • 目的是：to /in order to / so as to / for the sake of / for the purpose of
  • 通过做：via / by / through
  • 没有做: without

4、比较结构

  • more…than…

  • compare…with…

  • 对等

  • 错误：Compared with cities, people in rural areas are happier.

  • 正确：Compared with people in cities, those in rural areas are happier.
